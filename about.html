Nice to meet you!
I'm Jac.


I'm a product designer driven by curiosity and passion. I love solving complex problems with a data-driven mindset, an empathic approach, and an open mind.


I was born and raised in China, moved to Canada at the age of 15. I went through all those cliche struggles of having a language barrier and dealing with racism. I've been to more than 15 countries, and in 2017, I spent my summer in a small town called Karak in Malaysia, where I lived in a room of 30 double-beds with girls from the whole school. While we all know poverty exists and the ability to pursue higher education is a privilege, it felt different when I got to experience it in person. I went from hating myself for being Chinese to embracing who I am and be proud of it. Most importantly, I'm grateful for all these experiences that shaped me into the compassionate person that I am today.


So when I say I care, I meant it. I hope to create experiences that bring people happiness. I believe that together, we can make the world a better place.
