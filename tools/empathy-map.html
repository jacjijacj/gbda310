Step 1: Establish focus and goals

  1. Who is the person for the map?
  2. What is the design outcome?


Step 2: Capture the outside world

  1. The Says quadrant contains what the user says out loud in an interview or some other usability study. Ideally, it contains verbatim and direct quotes from research.
  2. The Thinks quadrant captures what the user is thinking throughout the experience.
  3. The Does quadrant encloses the actions the user takes.
  4. The Feels quadrant is the user’s emotional state, often represented as an adjective plus a short sentence for context
